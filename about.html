About Me

For as long as I can remember, I have always found value in beauty, aesthetics, and design. Things that are pleasing to the eye catch my attention. I am always scheming about ways of how I can make something look better. I was matching my father’s clothes for him by the age of 2, and by the age of 11, I knew how to do a full face of makeup. I took almost every art class available in high school. To one extent, you could say I am very creative, detail-oriented, intuitive, and attentive. I decided to pursue a career that exemplified these qualities.

On the other hand, I am also very logical, systematic, and analytical. I thoroughly enjoyed organizing data into spreadsheets and creating computer programs. This brought many challenges, especially when trying to choose careers.

I began my post-secondary education at the University of Wisconsin-Whitewater in the fall of 2017 pursuing a degree in Accounting and Business. This was a short-lived educational experience, as I dropped out after my first semester. But this was far from the end of my college days…

I decided I wanted to pursue a career in cosmetology. I thought, this might be a better fit. I enrolled at Aveda Institute of Beauty and Wellness in the fall of 2018, where I completed over 6 months of the cosmetology program. I then transferred to Moraine Park Technical College in June of 2019 to continue my cosmetology degree. I ended up retaking the first half of the program after the completion of the transferred portion of the program, as I didn’t feel completely prepared with the transfer process. After many months of training, I finally graduated with my cosmetology technical diploma in October 2020. I passed the Wisconsin State Board Exams with a 95% (written exam) and 98% (practical exam).

Excited and ready to start working, I began my cosmetology career at Supercuts in Germantown and Menomonee Falls, Wisconsin. I enjoyed my time with the Signature Two Company from November 2020 to July 2021. I gained lots of valuable experience at the two Supercuts locations. I then moved on to work at Healing Elements Day Spa beginning in October 2021. I am still currently at Healing Elements.

I had enjoyed computer programming since taking Introduction to Computer Science in high school. I decided to enroll in a class through the Bridges Library System. This class taught the fundamentals of Python 3 programming. I learned lots of introductory-level Python programming skills through this class such as Python 3 syntax, variables, mathematical computations, if statements, loops, functions, object-oriented programming, graphics with Tkinter, list structures, dictionaries, tuples, data files, and exception handling.

To take my computer programming interest even further, I enrolled in a Software Development Bootcamp through the University of Wisconsin-Madison. This 30-hour Introductory Course taught me many software development skills such as terminal commands, git and GitHub, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, object-oriented programming, and the Document Object Model.

I decided that I want to take my computer programming and software development skills and create a career out of it. So, I re-enrolled at Moraine Park in August 2022 in the Software Development program. I am currently working toward my Associates degree in Software Development at MPTC.
